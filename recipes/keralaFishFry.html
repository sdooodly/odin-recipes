Chicken Soup

Description

Fish Fry is one of the favorite dishes in Kerala.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Spices
  • Onion slices

How to make

  1. Clean the fish, make slices and marinate the fish with the spices.
  2. Shallow fry using any convenient oil on a pan, a no brainer, duhh.
  3. Serve with onion julianned(?) slices.
  4. Tada. Kazcho or bon apetit! :)
