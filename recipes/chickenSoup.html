Chicken Soup

Description

This homemade chicken soup recipe will soothe your soul. (;

Ingredients

  • Chicken: This homemade chicken soup starts with a 3-pound whole chicken.
  • Vegetables: You'll need carrots, celery, and an onion.
  • Seasonings: Season the soup with salt, pepper, and chicken bouillon granules.

How to make

  1. Make the stock by boiling the chicken and veggies until the meat is falling off the bone.
  2. Remove the chicken and veggies. Shred them.
  3. Return everything to the pot. Heat the soup and season to taste.
