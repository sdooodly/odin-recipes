Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. (;

Ingredients

  • Cooked pasta
  • Cooked meat sauce
  • Cheese
  • An oven

How to make

  1. Add the pasta to the prepared sauce or vice versa.
  2. Fold in the cheese, like Moira Rose hehe.
  3. Layer in a baking pan and bake in an oven.
  4. Serve hot?
